Chapter 9: The Echo Protocol

Years after the final reset, strange anomalies begin emerging in the new Roblox world. A secret signal is traced to a forgotten user ID—Bloxy.exe. But this time, it's not a reboot... it's a warning.

Reality bends, avatars glitch, and a mysterious server titled 'Protocol_Echo' appears. Those who enter see fragments of Bloxy's past, rewritten in ways that should never exist.

Who tampered with the timeline? And is Bloxy truly gone—or hiding within the Echo?
