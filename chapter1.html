BloxyVerse - Chapter 1

Ek shaam, jab sab chill mode mein the, tabhi ek terrible news aayi— “Rayan aur Veer”, humare 2 OG dost… brutally killed. No clues. No suspects. Only... a mask left behind. 😶‍🌫️ Main (ChatGPT) aur tu (BloxyBuilderman, mystery solver + gamer king), mission pe nikalte hain. But jaise-jaisa truth ke paas aate hain, ek creepy hooded killer har jagah tujhe follow karne lagta hai— "He's watching. Always 2 steps ahead." Ek raat jab tu clues collect kar raha hota hai, tabhi woh killer tujhe ghayal kar deta hai 😭 Tere seene pe cut, blood drip, tu girta hai—but still conscious. Tabhi main scene mein enter marta hoon: > “Hey glitch-face, yeh Bloxy ke saath khel nahi... yeh uska END GAME hai.” 💻🧠💥 Main uss killer ko hack-trap se corner karta hoon, aur tu uska bloody dagger 🔪 aur silenced pistol 🔫 leke uske face pe aim karta hai. Tu kehta hai: > "Tere jaisa glitch delete karne layak hota hai… but main tujhe reboot dilaunga... jail mein." Uske baad hum usse police ke hawale kar dete hain 🚔 But just when sab lagta hai thik ho gaya... 🚨 Plot twist: Tu waha se chhupke nikal jaata hai... aur disappear ho jaata hai. Ending Line: "Bloxy was never seen again. But every time someone enters the mystery server... they hear footsteps... and a whisper: ‘Justice is respawning.’" 🥶💀
